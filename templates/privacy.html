Data Integrity

Ding is a technical repository. We do not engage in broad-spectrum data collection. Our primary interest is technical metadata required for containerized orchestration and AI inference stability.

Local AI Inference

Crucially, all AI interactions (Ollama/RAG) occur within our local Kubernetes cluster. Your queries are never transmitted to external APIs or used for global model training. Private inference is a core architectural principle of this laboratory.

Cookie Policy

We use session-only technical cookies to maintain system state and AI context. These are non-tracking and expire immediately upon session termination.
