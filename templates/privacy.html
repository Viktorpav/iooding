Privacy & Security

How we protect your data through advanced infrastructure.

01. Uncompromising Infrastructure

Our platform is built on Talos OS and orchestrated via Kubernetes. This means the host operating system is fully immutable and ephemeral. By removing SSH access, console access, and all unnecessary packages from the host, we minimize the attack surface to near zero. Your data and interaction with our platform traverse through a highly secure, automated environment where manual tampering is cryptographically prohibited.

02. Local AI Inference

We believe your thoughts and queries belong to you. Our intelligent AI Assystent operates using 100% locally hosted models. No query you make is ever sent to third-party APIs like OpenAI, Anthropic, or Google. Everything is processed directly within our bare-metal infrastructure, ensuring absolute data sovereignty and privacy.

03. Strict Data Minimization

We only collect what is absolutely necessary for the platform to function. We do not employ invasive tracking scripts, cross-site trackers, or sell your data to advertisers. Our analytics are completely anonymized and self-hosted, ensuring that your reading habits and interactions remain confidential.

04. Open Source Transparency

Trust is earned through transparency. The core infrastructure manifests and configurations powering this platform are entirely open source. You can inspect how we route traffic, how we store data, and how we deploy our workloads. We invite the community to review our security posture because we have nothing to hide.
